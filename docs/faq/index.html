Q. Can I orient it vertically?
"Video Selection" You can change the orientation by tapping the "Device is rotating" icon in the bottom left corner of the screen.
Tap again to return to landscape orientation.

Q. Is it possible to make a touch sound?
You can set up the touch sound from "Settings > Settings on Video > Sound" in the app.
There are various types of touch sounds available, so you can choose the one you like best ‚ô™~

Q. Is it possible to show the touch points?
You can set the display settings for touch from "Settings > Settings on Video > Point" in the app.
You can also set the color and display size, so you can adjust it to your liking ‚ô™~

Q. Can I adjust the volume of the touch sound?
You cannot do this in the app. Please adjust the volume on your device.

Q. The home bar gets in the way during pseudo-play, making it difficult to play. Is there a home bar setting?
You can ignore the first home bar operation from "Settings > Video > Home Bar" in the app.

Q. I'm having trouble with the tap position and pause button being in the same place in some play videos. Can you fix this problem?
You can change the pause button to a long tap.
On the "Folder List" screen, you can select "Edit > Settings on Video > Pause button" to change to long tap.

Q. Where can I set up the app's touchline display?
On the "Folder List" screen, you can set it up by going to "Edit > Select Folder > App Type".

Q. Can you fix the sound shift?
There is no way to solve this problem within the app.
I do hope to respond to this someday, though.

Q. Is there a function to fast forward/rewind for a few seconds?
You can set it from the "Folder Settings > Fast forward/rewind button" button at the bottom left of the folder/video list screen.
You can also adjust the position of the display on the video screen and the number of seconds ‚ô™~

Q. Can I reposition or hide the rewind/fast forward button?
You can change or hide the position.
You can set it from the "Folder Settings > Fast forward/rewind button" button at the bottom left of the folder/video list screen.

Q. The resolution of YouTube videos is low. Is it possible for me to change it?
You can choose from 240p/360p/720p. The default setting is 360p.
You can configure the settings from "Common Settings > Video traffic" at the bottom left of the video list screen.

Q. When will the "xxx" app type be added?
It is difficult to support the recent iOS devices due to the increase in the number of different ratios. In addition, there are more and more apps that allow you to adjust the touchline position on the sound game app side, so it is not worth it.
This is why the frequency of updating the app types is low. However, that doesn't mean we don't want to increase the frequency.

Requests/bugs

Q. Where do I make my request?
Please send us your request from "Contact" in the app. We can't accept all requests, but we will consider them.
I'm also open to reviews on the AppStore. However, it is difficult to communicate with them, so we may not be able to understand their intentions.

Q. Where do I report bugs?
Please send us your request from "Contact" in the app.
Since it is difficult to communicate with AppStore reviews, it may not be possible to reproduce them and there may be a delay in response.

Q. YouTube videos are no longer playing in the app. Is this a bug?
It's most likely a bug. Please contact us from "Contact" in the app, including the URL of the video that is not playing.

Q. Videos from my camera roll are no longer playing in the app. Is this a bug?
Deleting a video from the camera roll will stop it from playing.
If you choose to save photos to iCloud, they will not be playable at a later date. Please register the video uploaded to iCloud to the application.
If you have videos in your camera roll but they do not play, try restarting the app/device to see if that solves the problem.
